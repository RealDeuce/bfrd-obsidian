# Currency
An average coin weighs less than half an ounce. A pouch of 50 coins weighs 1 pound.
###### Currency Conversion
| Coin          | CP    | SP   | GP    | PP      |
| ------------- | ----- | ---- | ----- | ------- |
| Copper (cp)   | 1     | 1/10 | 1/100 | 1/1,000 |
| Silver (sp)   | 10    | 1    | 1/10  | 1/100   |
| Gold (gp)     | 100   | 10   | 1     | 1/10    |
| Platinum (pp) | 1,000 | 100  | 10    | 1       |
